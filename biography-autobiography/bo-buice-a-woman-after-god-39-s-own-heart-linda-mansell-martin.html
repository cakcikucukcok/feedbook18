Linda Mansell Martin, a native of Roswell, Georgia, is a 1992 graduate of Trinity University of Deerfield, Illinois, Atlanta Extension, with a BA Degree in Communications. In addition, she is a Certifi ed Precept Bible Teacher having taught Bible Studies for over 25 years. She is the author of a family cookbook, A Measure of Love, which has been widely distributed and a Bible Study on Marriage, Love Beyond Measure.

Linda is married to Alan Martin, also of Roswell, her husband of 42 years, and they have three married sons: Russell Alan (Rusty) Thomas Anthony (Tony) and Ryan Coleman Martin. They are the proud grandparents of eight wonderful blessings.

This is the story of Lindas Spiritiual Mother, Bo Buice, and her famous son, Ervin, (Jason Byce, theatrical name), whom Linda knew and loved throughout their school years together. It is a story of triumph over tragedy and of the faithfulness of God to His own.
