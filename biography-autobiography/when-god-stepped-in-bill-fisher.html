After a devastating car accident that killed his wife, Yvonne, and left author Bill Fisher severely wounded, he realized that this time, his life had just been spared from death for the ninth time. In When God Stepped In, Fisher shares how his life has been punctuated with a series of miraculous acts of the Lord.

In this memoir, he narrates a series of encounters that have confirmed his faith in the power of God including healings, rescues, protection, shield, and provision. Fisher tells how God has allowed him to get into many unexpected, dangerous situations throughout his life and then has rescued him in often spectacular ways.
