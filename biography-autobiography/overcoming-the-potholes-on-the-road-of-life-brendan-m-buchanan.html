No one is exempt from having potholes on their road of life, whether they are a king, or queen, or an ordinary person. Navigating a pothole or two in lifes journey is a way of learning to make yourself stronger to face the future. In Overcoming the Potholes on the Road of Life, author Brendan M. Buchanan reviews his sometimes difficult past and shares some of the potholes hes encountered along the way. From the day of his birth in the latter half of 1946 in Mackay, North Queensland, to the last day of 2016, Buchanan narrates his life story and how he has dealt with his challenges, including three marriages and a host of medical problems. In this memoir, Buchanan chronicles a variety of experiences, including information about his upbringing, his family, employment, service in the military, travels, relationships, and more. Overcoming the Potholes on the Road of Life, shares Buchanans goal of becoming an achiever and to be dazzled by the road ahead.
