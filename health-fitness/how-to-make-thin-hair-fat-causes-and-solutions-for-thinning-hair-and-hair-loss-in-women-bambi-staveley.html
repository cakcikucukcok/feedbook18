The culmination of 4 years of research and personal experience with female thinning hair

Hair loss in varying degrees affects millions of women the world over. Yet this is a subject almost never discussed. Bambi Staveley, hair loss writer and former nurse who has herself been a hair loss sufferer, has spent many years researching the many causes and solutions. For many women thin or thinning hair creeps up as part of the aging process. Wrinkles and aching joints are all common, if not unwanted, outcomes of the aging process, but not the loss of our once gorgeous, thick crowning glory. For the vast majority of women – eighty percent by the age of sixty – a desperate search for the cause and any solution becomes a common pursuit. This carefully researched book describes all the major causes of hair thinning with dozens of tips and solutions, all tried by the author and thousands of her online followers.There is no need for a women who feels self conscious about visible thinning hair to be embarrassed any longer. Certainly not once she’s read this book.
