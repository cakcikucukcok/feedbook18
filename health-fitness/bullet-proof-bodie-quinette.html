Many have experienced...many have witnessed...many have often heard about...

Some gave up...or lost their minds...or died trying to figure it out...

But, only a few have actually gone through - ‘unscathed and unmarked’ – 

the sometimes tiresome and often unwelcomed beatings from life storms.

Bullet Proof is a personal testimony creatively written to openly explore nine traumatic and real-life experiences endured by the author – all of which she was able to survive, heal and learn from, thanks to God’s unfailing love 

and ever-present existence in her life. 

This book is an encouraging “must read” for  adolescents, teens and adults who have gone through and/or find themselves going through one of life’s storms: child/domestic abuse, teenage pregnancy, sexual exploitations, 

low self esteem, failed relationships, divorce, fake friends, bankruptcy or spiritual confusion. 

DIFFERENT. REAL. INSPIRING.  Written from a woman’s perspective, but all – women and men – can benefit from its poetic and soul-inspiring content. May your spirit be lifted...your heart be touched...your life be changed!
