This book covers answers to seven major problems that effect millions of people around the world.

Each topic: Global Warming, High-Speed transit, Welfare poverty, Renewable Clean Energy, Coastline Stabilization, Taxing Calories, and Economic Democracy, is addressed separately.

Some of the issues will take years to fully implement the answers while some could be started right away with immediate results in any small endeavor.

It is not a full cookbook down to the final details but does give a broad approach to each topic with significant insights into what the problems are and how to go about making things better.
