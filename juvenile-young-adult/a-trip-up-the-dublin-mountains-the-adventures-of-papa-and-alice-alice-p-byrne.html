A Trip Up The Dublin Mountains is the first in a series of stories hailing from the Emerald Isle. From the peaks of mountains, the shore of sea side towns, the bustle of fruit markets and the farm fresh smell of the countryside, there is much to see and learn. Every time is a new adventure! Some Irish words have been used throughout the story to give kids a little taste of the traditional language while having fun trying to pronounce them!
