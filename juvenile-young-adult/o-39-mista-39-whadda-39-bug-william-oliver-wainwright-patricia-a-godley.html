The story of O’ Mista’ Whadda’ Wata’ Bug! is a fictional account of these two perky little bugs who had made their home inside of this most beautifully crafted, handmade rug.

Life for them was pretty snug indeed! That is, until the rug where they stayed was shipped off to a place called Twitterville. There was absolutely no way they could have ever imagined (with the events that followed ) that in this small town of Twitterville, their little bug world would be turned literally, upside down!

Their lifelong friendship is soon put to the test. One of the bugs has a very strange dream and wanted to pursue it. But when he told his little friend about it, the friend tries to persuade him otherwise. What becomes of our two little bug characters? The telling is in the tale!
