OUT-OF-BODY TRAVEL MAGAZINE: The ninth issue of the 'The Out-of-Body Travel Foundation Journal' focus on Maharaj Chawan Singh, Sant Mat Master (Sikh). Huzur Maharaj Sawan Singh, his grandfather and one of the original Masters in the Sant Mat tradition came to me in the mystical state and asked me to release a book entitled 'At the Feet of the Masters,' Divine Providence led a former student and initiate of his grandson to contact me. In our 'Question and Answer' Section, Skyler Grimes asks about the crushing sensation of Vibrational Raisings, an important phenomenon to understand in the mechanism of Out-of-Body Travel. And In our 'Different Voices' section, we share Charan Singh's teaching on 'The Path of Light and Sound,' and an anonymous initiate shares his 'Peak Meditation Experience' as guided by Maharaj Chawan Singh. We conclude with Sawan Singh's Meditation Lesson and Rai Salig Ram's, 2nd Guru in the Radhasoami Lineage HIDAYATNAMA - Esoteric Teachings.
