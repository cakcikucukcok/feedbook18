ABSOLUTE DISSOLUTION OF BODY AND MIND: BOOK 4 OF THE MYSTERIES OF THE REDEMPTION SERIES (OUT-OF-BODY TRAVEL): In 'Absolute Dissolution of body and Mind', you will meet through out-of-body travel the great prophets, saints, mystics and sages from throughout the ages and every world religion who come in unity to teach the soul of itself, so that it may unite with the royal road they have tread. Along the way, you will travel out-of-body to heavenly, hellish and purgatorial realms to learn more about the mechanism of such places along with attaining to a greater understanding of the good and evil that lies within each one of us. Each of these books not only gives practical guidance on how to achieve out-of-body experiences, but each volume expands further on achieving higher states and handling the many situations that you come across when traveling out-of-body.
