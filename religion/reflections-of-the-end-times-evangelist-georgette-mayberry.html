This book was written because I found out how important it is to give your life to Jesus. Now I understand why it is important to accept Him as your Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. Not only was I blinded, but I could have been dead and in hell. I thank and praise God for His grace and mercy on me; for enlightening me, caring for me and for giving me hope. For loving me and for who I am. I thank Him for teaching me the truth. For letting me be able to write this book. I want the world to know young and old, no matter what is wrong or went wrong in your life, you can make it.
