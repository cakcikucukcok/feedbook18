This athletics unit focuses on improving speed strength endurance. After warm-up and a short game, five athletics exercises train the various groups of thigh muscles by moving forwards, backwards, and to the side. This training unit is very intense and can thus be incorporated in preparation periods or season breaks. The training unit consists of the following key exercises: - Warm-up/Stretching (individual exercise: 10 minutes/total time: 10 minutes) - Short game (10/20) - Athletics training (15/35) - Athletics training (15/50) - Athletics training (15/65) - Athletics training (15/80) - Athletics training (10/90) Total training time: 90 minutes Difficulty level: Top level (competitive area)
